Easy Chicken Piccata

This is one of the best Chicken Piccata you will find out there. Its Lemony, caper-topped, and buttery. It taste amazing and its easy to make.

You will need :

  • 2 - Large boneless/skinless chicken breasts

  • 1 - Cup flour

  • 1/2 - Butter

  • 1/2 - White wine

  • 3 - Medium sized lemons (juiced)

  • 2 - Tablespoons drained capers

  • 2 - Tablespoons parsley (chopped)

  • Salt / Pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. Slice chicken into 4 pieces. Pound each piece 1/4 - inch thin. Flour chicken pieces on both sides.

  2. Melt butter in large pan over medium heat. Place chicken pieces into the pan and season with salt and pepper. Turn chicken over when nearly browned and season the other side. When both sides are browned remove the chicken from pan and place onto a plate.

  3. In the same pan, add wine and lemon juice to scrape any browned bits in the pan. Simmer till thickened about 5-7 minutes. Add capers and then pour sauce over plated chicken. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
