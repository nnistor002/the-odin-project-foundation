Easy Eggs

This will show you a way to get perfect eggs depending on how soft or hard you want them.

You will need :

  • Any amount - Large eggs

  • Salt

  • Water

Steps :

  1. Bring to a boil salt water in a pot, large enough to fit eggs with about 1 inch of water above the eggs.

    • Runny - Add eggs and cook them for 5 - 6 minutes

    • Soft - Add eggs and cook them for 7 - 8 minutes

    • Hard - Add eggs and cook them for 9 - 10 minutes

  2. Season, Serve and Enjoy
