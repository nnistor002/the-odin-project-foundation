Light and Easy Greek Potato Salad

Light, easy and hard to mess up Greek potato salad.

You will need :

  • 12 - Red potatoes

  • 1/4 - Cup chopped green onion

  • 1/4 - Cup olive oil

  • 1/4 - Cup red wine vinegar

  • 1-1/2 - Teaspoon lemon juice

  • 1/2 - Teaspoon galic powder

  • 1/2 - Teaspoon onion powder

  • 1/2 - Teaspoon coarse salt

  • 1/2 - Teaspoon black pepper

  • 1/4 - Teaspoon dried oregano

  • 1/4 - Teaspoon white sugar

  • 1/4 - Teaspoon dried rosemary (crumbled)

  • 1 - Pinch ground red pepper

Steps :

  1. Place red potatos in salt water and bring to a boild. Reduce heat to medium-low, simmer until tender (about 25 minutes). Drain and chill in freezer until cold (about 30 - 35 minutes).

  2. After chilled, slice potatos and place in large salad bowl. Add green onions and toss the potatos with green onion while adding olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, oregano, suger, rosemary and red pepper. Serve immediatly or chill for later.
